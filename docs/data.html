📊 Data Strategy & Sources

Our project focuses on the evolution of Industrial Engineering (IE) departments in Turkey between 2018 and 2025. To ensure scientific accuracy and reproducibility, we retrieve our data directly from official sources using automated tools.

Primary Data Source

The data is sourced from the YÖK Atlas “Lisans Atlası” module, which provides official statistics on university placements in Turkey.

Extraction Tool

We use the thestats R package, developed by M. Çavuş and O. Aydın, to query the YÖK Atlas database. This allows us to maintain a dynamic and updateable workflow.

Preprocessing Steps

To prepare the data for analysis, we followed these steps:

Filtering: We isolated “Industrial Engineering” programs, excluding other engineering branches.

Handling NAs: Departments that did not fill their quota or were newly established (lacking base scores) were identified. We removed these from trend analyses to prevent statistical bias.

Standardization: City names and university types were standardized to ensure consistent grouping during the visualization phase.
